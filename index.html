Facilities

COTTAGES

a shaded outdoor area with multiple wooden cottages or huts arranged in rows beneath tall, leafy trees. These huts have nipa or metal roofs and are equipped with benches, commonly found in beach or picnic resorts in tropical areas.

LIFE GUARD STATION

Its elevated position allows lifeguards a clear view of the beach for better surveillance and quicker response times to emergencies. This improves beach safety for swimmers and other beachgoers.
